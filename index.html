    One Step Forward

    November 23, 2023

    Video games spent enough time in my head for the creation of this site to feel almost inevitable. Or maybe it has to do with the year ending?

    In any case, here it is! I'm not entirely sure of what the goal for this rambling is, but for now I hope that someday it could be fun to look back at these old thoughts and remember the good, the bad, and the Baldur's Gates of it all.
